Two people, one drafting table.

We met at a risograph workshop in 2020 and started Mochi a year later, after Lin spilled a coffee on Aria's sketchbook and Aria said "draw something in it." That ruined sketchbook became our first client deck.

Today we make illustration sets, brand identities, and short motion pieces — usually for small studios, wellness apps, and a few publishing friends. We work slowly and on purpose; one project at a time.

Four steps, usually six weeks.

Step 01

Hello

A 30-minute call. We ask too many questions. You ask whatever you like. Free, no scope yet.

Step 02

Sketch

Quick pencil sketches, fast iterations. You see three directions in week one. We pick one.

Step 03

Polish

Final illustrations + delivery in your preferred formats. SVG, PNG, Lottie — whichever fits.

Step 04

Bounce

If you want motion: 1-2 short animations after launch. Otherwise we send a postcard.

Got something to bounce? Send a note.
